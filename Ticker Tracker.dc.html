StrategyLive algo health — P&L, risk and execution at a glance
How to read the Strategy tab×
Built for a 30-second health check: read it top-to-bottom, left-to-right. If nothing is red, your algo is healthy — deeper detail is a click away.
1
KPI bannerStrategy scorecard. Sharpe = return per unit of risk (higher is better, >1 is good). Max Drawdown = worst peak-to-trough loss. Win Rate & Risk/Reward = how often and how big you win. Trend Strength tells you if the market is trending (algo's friend) or ranging.
2
Equity CurveYour cumulative P&L over time (green/red area) vs a benchmark (dashed). You want your line above the dashed one and rising.
3
Risk & Execution (right)Circuit Breakers are your auto-stop limits — the bar fills as you approach the daily-loss cap and the panel goes red at the limit. System Health traffic lights watch your broker connection, rate limits and margin. Execution Quality shows how cleanly orders fill (latency, fill rate, slippage).
4
Active PositionsEvery open trade — side (Long/Short), entry, current value, and unrealized P&L. Click a row to open that ticker's full dashboard.
